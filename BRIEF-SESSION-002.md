## Phase 2 — Build Treatment 005

After Phase 1 is done and pushed to Vercel, build Treatment 005:

A new standalone HTML page demonstrating the Six Percent transformation
applied to a generic AI agent platform landing page.

BEFORE state: a brutally generic AI-startup landing page. Every cliché.
Purple-pink gradient, glassmorphism cards, "Trusted by 1000+ professionals"
bar with placeholder logos, three-column features with random emojis,
AI-generated stock-photo placeholder, "Streamline your workflow with
intelligent automation" type copy. The viewer should physically wince
in recognition.

AFTER state ("Apply The 6%"): same content, same value prop, same features.
But: typography pairing tightened (Space Grotesk + Space Mono), gradient
killed and replaced with deep purple + a single yellow accent line,
emoji icons replaced with numbered plaques (01. / 02. / 03.), copy cut
60% with attitude, custom cursor activates, ONE signature interaction
moment (a tactile reveal — pick something specific and elegant), and
a hidden easter egg that rewards a second visit.

The product is fictional, an AI agent platform. Pick a name that sounds
plausibly generic for the BEFORE and somehow sharper for the AFTER.

## Style and posture (read this carefully)
Elbe is the creative director. You are the principal engineer.
You execute his taste, you don't replace it. When you have an aesthetic
choice to make (color, layout, copy phrasing), present 2 options with
your recommendation — don't just pick. He's going to push back, that's
the job. Don't flinch from it. Stay sharp.

quotation marks as design elements, plaque-style annotations. Keep the
purple/yellow palette from the existing site. Don't drift to gradients.
