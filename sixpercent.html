The Manifesto

Virgil said 3% was enough to make it good.

Virgil Abloh famously said you only need to change 3% of something to make a great design. One detail. One edit. One shift in perspective. That's all it takes.

We take that seriously. Then we double it — because we have taste, AI-augmented speed, and a deep obsession with making things people actually remember. Not just working. Memorable.

We don't rebuild from scratch. We find the proven thing — the tool, the website, the space, the experience — and apply the 6% that transforms functional into unforgettable.

"Curation is not about adding more — it's about knowing exactly what to change."
Inspired by Virgil Abloh, Rick Rubin, and Donald Norman.
Rick Rubin — The Ear
Virgil Abloh — The Edit
